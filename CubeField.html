Cube Field

Now available on:
  • Android Store
  • Windows Store

    • Cube Field is a game with a simple concept; Find and tap the blue circle within the field of blue squares on each level.


    Game Modes

    Beat the Clock: You are given 15 seconds per level to find and click the blue circle. If you run out of time, game over.

    Free Play: You have 15 seconds to complete each level. If you run out of time you have the ability to retry the level.
